## Definition

A **soft block** is a subtle way of limiting contact on social media without fully blocking someone.

## Meaning and Usage

This may include muting, unfollowing, or quietly removing someone to avoid confrontation.

## Origin

The term emerged as platforms added more nuanced privacy and restriction tools.
