## Definition

**Breadcrumbing** is a dating behavior where someone gives minimal attention to keep another person interested without committing.

## Meaning and Usage

It often involves occasional texts or vague promises that prevent closure but offer no real progress.

## Origin

The term comes from *Hansel and Gretel*, where breadcrumbs were used to mark a path.

## Examples

- *He keeps breadcrumbing her instead of making plans.*
- *Breadcrumbing creates emotional confusion.*
- *Random check-ins can be a sign of breadcrumbing.*
