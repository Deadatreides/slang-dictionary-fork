## Definition

**Love bombing** is overwhelming someone with excessive affection or attention to gain emotional influence.

## Meaning and Usage

It often appears early in relationships and may later turn into control or withdrawal.

## Origin

The term originated in psychology and studies of manipulative group behavior.

## Examples

- *The intensity felt like love bombing.*
- *Love bombing can hide controlling tendencies.*
- *Fast attachment is sometimes a warning sign.*
