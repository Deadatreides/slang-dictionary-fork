## Definition

A **situationship** is an undefined romantic connection without clear labels or commitment.

## Meaning and Usage

It often involves dating-like behavior without exclusivity or long-term plans.

## Origin

The word blends “situation” and “relationship.”

## Examples

- *They’ve been stuck in a situationship for months.*
- *Situationships can feel emotionally draining.*
- *She wants clarity, not another situationship.*
